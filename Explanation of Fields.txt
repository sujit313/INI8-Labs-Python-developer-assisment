Explanation of Fields:

ID: Auto-incrementing integer, serving as the primary key.
Name: VARCHAR(100) for storing the user's name.
Email: VARCHAR(255) for storing the user's email address, with a UNIQUE constraint to ensure uniqueness.
DateOfBirth: DATE for storing the user's date of birth.
Gender: VARCHAR(10) for storing the user's gender.
Address: TEXT for storing the user's address.
PhoneNumber: VARCHAR(20) for storing the user's phone number.
RegistrationDate: TIMESTAMP for storing the date and time of registration, with a default value of the current timestamp.
